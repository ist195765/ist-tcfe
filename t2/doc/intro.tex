The sinusoidal voltage source $V_s$ varies in time exactly as it follows:

\begin{equation}
V_{s}(t) = V_{s}u(-t) + sin(2 \pi f t)u(t)
\label{equation1}
\end{equation}
Where
\begin{equation}
 u(t)= e
    \begin{cases}
     0 & \text{t $<$ 0}\\
      1 & \text{t $\geq$ 0}
    \end{cases} 
\label{eq:tt}      
\end{equation}
